Beauty & Style

Rich Tym Luxe is more than just a beauty salon - we're a destination where beauty dreams come true. Located in the heart of Ho, Ghana, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the beauty industry, offering a comprehensive range of services to help our clients look and feel their absolute best.

Our team of skilled professionals is passionate about beauty and dedicated to delivering exceptional results. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll experience the warmth of Ghanaian hospitality combined with world-class beauty services.

Our Story

A Journey of Beauty & Excellence

Built on Passion

Rich Tym Luxe was founded with a simple vision: to bring world-class beauty services to Ho, Ghana. We recognized a need for a premium beauty destination that combines professional expertise with personalized service.

Over the years, we've grown from a small beauty studio to a full-service beauty destination. Our commitment to quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction has earned us a loyal clientele and made us one of the most recommended beauty salons in the region.

We continuously update our techniques and services to stay current with global beauty trends while maintaining the authentic Ghanaian touch that makes us unique.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional beauty services that enhance our clients' natural beauty while boosting their confidence. We strive to create a welcoming environment where every client feels valued and leaves feeling beautiful inside and out.

Our Vision

To be the leading beauty brand in Ghana, known for professionalism, quality, and innovation. We aim to set the standard for beauty services in the region while maintaining our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction.
